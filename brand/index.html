Quick answers

Common questions.

Is it really free?

Yes, Demohub is free for brands forever. Retailers pay for the platform; brands get the profile, dashboard, and one-click booking at no cost. Brands do pay a flat $5 booking fee per confirmed demo, collected at the point of booking.

How do I find retailers on Demohub?

Most brands come in through retailers they already work with. Your retailer contact shares their public booking link with you, and you book from there. Once you have an account, you can also discover new Demohub retailers through your dashboard as they come online.

What if I edit my info?

Your default profile updates apply to all future bookings. Retailers can still see and edit their own copy of your contact info for past bookings, you're not overwriting their records.

Can retailers see what I do at other retailers?

No. Strict privacy walls. Retailers only see brand data tied to their own bookings, never anything cross-retailer.

What happens if I already have demos at a Demohub retailer?

They stay right where they are. When you sign up for a brand account using the same email your retailer has on file for you, all your existing demos and history link automatically. Nothing is lost, you just get one dashboard to manage everything going forward.
