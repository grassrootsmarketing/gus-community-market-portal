What does Demohub actually do?

Demohub is the demo operations platform for independent retailers. Brands book demo slots at your stores through a public link. You approve the bookings, the schedule populates itself, and you get clean monthly statements at the end of every month. Compliance docs, contact info, and revenue tracking happen alongside.

Who is this for?

Independent grocery, wine, beer, and specialty retailers, anyone with 1 to ~30 stores who hosts in-store brand demos and is currently using spreadsheets and email threads to manage the chaos. Single-store owners get value too; multi-store operators get the most.

How much does it cost?

Free 30-day trial, no credit card to start. After that, pricing tracks your demo volume, we want it to feel like a no-brainer for stores hosting 5+ demos per month. Specific pricing will be on this site soon; meanwhile, the free trial gives you the whole product.

Do I have to enter all my brand contacts myself?

No, that's the point. Brands book through your public link, fill out their own info, and their contact + compliance details land in your admin automatically. You can add brands manually if you want to invite a specific one, but you don't have to.

Can I set different hours for each store?

Yes. Each store has its own availability schedule (open days, time windows, max demos per slot). You can also set one schedule and apply it to all your stores with a single click, handy if your stores keep consistent hours.

How do brands pay the demo fee?

Stripe Checkout integration is in development, once you have your Stripe account connected, brands pay the demo fee upfront when they book, and the funds land in your account. Until that's live, fees are tracked in your admin (so your monthly statement is accurate), and you collect via your existing process.

What's the cancellation policy?

Up to each retailer, every store sets their own policy. You'll see it on the booking page before you submit, and it's listed in your confirmation email. Most retailers offer some grace period; some are non-refundable. When in doubt, reach out to the store directly.

Does Demohub work with my Google Calendar / Apple Calendar / Outlook?

Yes, it works with all the major calendar apps. Subscribe once and confirmed demos show up automatically.

Can my whole team access the admin?

Yes. You can invite team members to your store's admin from the Settings page, each one gets their own sign-in. You decide who can edit (admin) and who's view-only.

Where's my data stored? Is it private?

Your data is hosted on enterprise-grade infrastructure in US-West with strict access controls. Each retailer's data is fully isolated, other stores can never see your brands, bookings, or contacts. We never sell, share, or train on your data. Email david@demohubhq.com with any privacy questions.
