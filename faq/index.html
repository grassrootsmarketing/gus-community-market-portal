Frequently asked questions

Quick answers

Everything you need to know about running demos through Demohub — for retailers and brands. Can't find your answer? Email us.

For retailers
How does Demohub make money?

We charge retailers a flat monthly subscription based on how many stores you run — Solo ($49/mo), Starter ($99/mo), Growth ($199/mo), or Enterprise ($399/mo). Pricing is per-retailer, not per-demo, so the more demos you run, the better the deal.

You also collect a per-demo fee from each brand (you set the amount in Settings). We charge a small Demohub platform fee on top of Stripe's processing fee — the rest goes straight to your bank.

When do I get paid for demos?

Once you've connected your bank in Settings → Bank & payouts (uses Stripe Express), demo payments land in your account within 2 business days of each demo, minus Stripe + Demohub fees.

Brands are charged when they book — funds are held until 24 hours after the demo, then released. If a demo is cancelled inside your cancellation policy window, the brand is refunded automatically.

Can I require a Certificate of Insurance (COI) from every brand?

Yes — and it's required by default. Brands can't request a booking at your store without an uploaded, unexpired COI.

You get an automatic warning 90 days before any brand's COI expires, and so do they (30, 14, and 3 days out). The verified brand badge on the booking page only appears once a brand has a current COI on file.

What if a brand cancels last minute?

You set your own cancellation policy in Settings → Cancellation policy. The default is: full refund 7+ days out, 50% refund 3–7 days out, no refund inside 72 hours.

Brands see this policy at booking time, agree to it before payment, and refunds run automatically through Stripe.

How do I add my team?

Settings → Team → Invite. They get a magic-link email — no password required. Owners and admins can manage everything; viewers can see the calendar and demo list but can't book or change settings.

You can have up to 10 owners/admins per retailer, and unlimited viewers.

Can I sync demos to Google Calendar / Apple Calendar / Outlook?

Yes — Settings → Calendar feed has a personal .ics URL you can subscribe to from any calendar app. New bookings appear automatically; cancellations remove themselves. One-time setup, no ongoing sync needed.

For brands
How do I book a demo at a retailer?

Visit the retailer's public booking page (the link they shared with you) and pick a date, store, and time slot. You'll need a brand account with a current COI uploaded — Demohub blocks bookings without one.

You pay the demo fee at booking. Funds aren't released to the retailer until 24 hours after the demo, so you're protected if anything goes sideways.

What does the verified brand badge mean?

The green checkmark on the booking page means: (1) your COI is uploaded and current, (2) you've completed at least one demo through Demohub, and (3) you're in good standing (no cancellation history outside policy).

Retailers can choose to only accept verified brands. Most do.

How do I upload my Certificate of Insurance?

Brand portal → Profile → Insurance. PDF only, up to 10 MB. We extract the expiration date automatically (you can override it if we get it wrong). You'll get renewal reminders 30, 14, and 3 days before expiry.

Technical
Is Demohub secure? Where's my data stored?

Data lives in Supabase (Postgres) hosted in US-East. All connections are TLS 1.3. Magic-link tokens are single-use and expire in 24 hours. Sessions are stored client-side in localStorage and expire after 30 days of inactivity.

Payment data is handled entirely by Stripe (PCI Level 1) — we never see or store card numbers, bank accounts, or routing numbers.

What if I forget my login?

Go to demohubhq.com/signin, enter your email, and we'll send you a fresh magic link. Same for brand portal at demohubhq.com/brand/signin. No passwords to remember, ever.

Can I export my data?

Yes — admin → Demos → Export gives you CSV of all demos. Admin → Settings → Export account gives you a full JSON dump of stores, team, bookings, settings, and policies. Email us if you need anything else; we'll send within 48 hours.
