The platform

Built around how independent retailers actually work.

Not a CRM. Not a scheduling app. A purpose-built operations layer for retailers who care about brand discovery and shopper experience.

Booking that runs itself

Brands self-serve on your portal. Pick a store, pick a time, sign the agreement, done. You wake up to a full week.

Compliance, automated

Track COIs, food handler certs, signed agreements: per brand, per contact. Auto-alerts before anything expires.

Numbers that move things

Revenue per store. Top brands. Seasonal trends. Real metrics, no manual exports. Make next quarter's plan in twenty minutes.
