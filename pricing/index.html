Pricing questions.

What counts as a "demo"?

A confirmed booking in your Demohub admin. Pending bookings, declined bookings, and cancelled bookings don't count. You're only billed once you click Confirm.

What if I don't charge brands a demo fee?

You owe Demohub nothing. The per-demo fee is calculated from what you charge the brand — if the demo fee is under $10, it rounds down to $0. Charge nothing, owe nothing.

Do brands pay anything?

No. Demohub is free forever for brands. They get a profile, a dashboard, and one-click booking at every Demohub retailer at no cost. Retailers pay for the platform.

Is there a free trial?

Yes — every paid plan starts with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start. You'll get a reminder before the trial ends, and you can switch plans, downgrade, or cancel any time.

Can I switch plans?

Anytime. Upgrades take effect immediately and we pro-rate the difference. Downgrades take effect at your next billing date so you keep the features you paid for. Cancel any time with one click.

Annual vs. monthly — what's the difference?

Annual saves you 17% — that's about two months free. Solo is $480/yr (vs. $588 monthly), Starter is $984/yr (vs. $1,188 monthly), Growth is $1,992/yr (vs. $2,388 monthly). Per-demo fees are billed monthly either way.
