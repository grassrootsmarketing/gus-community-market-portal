Pricing questions.

What counts as a "demo"?

A confirmed booking in your Demohub admin. Pending bookings, declined bookings, and cancelled bookings don't count. You're only billed once you click Confirm.

What if I don't charge brands a demo fee?

You owe Demohub nothing either way. The retailer never pays a per-demo fee, only the Pro subscription if you're on that tier. The $5 booking fee is paid by the brand at checkout, on top of whatever demo fee you set, including $0.

Do brands pay anything?

Yes. Brands pay a flat $5 booking fee per confirmed demo, plus whatever demo fee the retailer charges. No subscription, no account cost. They get a profile, a dashboard, and one-click booking at every Demohub retailer.

Is there a free trial?

Solo is free forever. Pro comes with a 14-day free trial. You'll get a reminder before the Pro trial ends, and you can switch plans, downgrade, or cancel any time.

Can I switch plans?

Anytime. Upgrades take effect immediately and we pro-rate the difference. Downgrades take effect at your next billing date so you keep the features you paid for. Cancel any time with one click.

Annual vs. monthly, what's the difference?

Annual saves you 17% on Pro, that's about two months free ($299.88/yr vs. $359.88 monthly). Solo tier is free either way. Brand $5 booking fees are handled by Stripe at the time of each demo.
